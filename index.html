Safety Guide

What is Digital Arrest?
A digital arrest is a cyber scam where criminals pretend to be police officers or government officials and falsely accuse someone of being involved in a crime. They pressure the victim through phone or video calls and create fear by saying the person will be arrested.

What Happens?
Scammers may show fake documents, uniforms, or IDs to look official. They often force the victim to stay on the call while they conduct a fake “investigation.” During this time, they demand money, bank details, or personal information to supposedly “clear the case.”

How to Stay Safe:
• Real authorities do not arrest people through phone or video calls.
• Never share personal or banking information with unknown callers.
• End suspicious calls immediately.
• Verify the claim with official authorities before taking any action.
